# Starter Vault

The packaged starter vault is a minimal public template, not a project truth dump.

## Included

- folder layout for `00 Wiki/` and `10 Studio/`
- starter navigation pages
- templates copied from the original internal vault
- a few sample pages that demonstrate the artifact contracts

## Excluded

- private or project-specific truth pages
- live execution plans and logs from the source project
- user-state Obsidian workspace files

## Expected Customization

- replace sample pages with your real truth
- adapt terminology to your project
- keep the folder responsibilities stable unless you also change the scripts
